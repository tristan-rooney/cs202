Tee Time

  • History

    This store was formed in 2006 by Mac Layton and his close friend Kyle Varan. They both saw the need for higher quality t-shirt that could be custom made to fit their customers perfectly. They started the business by renting out an old warehouse and using a hot iron to press onto blank shirts. In 2007 they were not making a lot of money and had to stop production for 3 months. After hearing of this, a friend invested in the businesses advertisement initiative and boosted sales tenfold. Once they gained more business they needed a better way to manage their orders so a website was created and they now ship all over the US. Tee Time started out with only two employees in one state and has grown to 30 employees in two states. This has all occurred in only a 12 year span and is currently growing at a 13% rate every year!

  • Philosophy

    Our philosophy at Tee Time is to provide customers with the best quality at the best price. We know how frustrating it can be to go to the store as well as the pains associated with ordering clothes online. Because of this, we have a 100% guarantee on all of our products and if it doesn't fit or you just don't like it, send it back on us!

  • People

    Our people over here at Tee Time consist of many characters. Perhaps the biggest character of us all is our founder Mac Layton. Next is our VP Kyle Varan, him and Mac started the business back in 2006 and are sill best of friends. As for the rest of the team, Tee Time spreads between two states with 30 employees of all walks of life. We have one of the highest rated employee satisfaction ratings in the country!
